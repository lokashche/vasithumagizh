# VasithuMagizh 📖

**படிக்கிற மகிழ்ச்சியை பகிர்வோம்!** *(Let's share the joy of reading!)*

An open-source platform from Chennai that lets you **exchange, rent, give away, and sell books** — bringing the joy of reading to every corner of namma city.

## What is VasithuMagizh?

VasithuMagizh (*Vasithu* = read, *Magizh* = joy) is a community-driven book platform inspired by Chennai's rich literary culture — from the pavement booksellers on Triplicane High Road to the Sunday book bazaar at Mylapore.

**The idea is simple:** books should move, not sit. VasithuMagizh connects readers with readers — no middlemen, no hefty commissions.

### What you can do

- **Exchange** — Swap books with fellow readers across the city
- **Rent** — Borrow textbooks and novels at prices cheaper than a filter kaapi
- **Giveaway** — Pass on books you've loved to someone who'll love them next
- **Sell** — Find buyers who actually appreciate your collection, from Kalki to Kafka
